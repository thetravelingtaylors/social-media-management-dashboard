End-of-Month Goals Review

At the end of every month, go back to the goals spreadsheet and review what was set at the start of the month. For each goal, mark whether or not we hit it — and note why.

This isn't just bookkeeping. The "why" is what tells us what to keep doing, what to drop, and what to push harder on next month.

When tasks are done — fill the hours ✈️

If everything assigned is complete and there's still time left in the week, do one of the following. The goal is to use up all your hours productively, and always check your assigned tasks in Blue first to make sure nothing's been missed.

  • i.
    Watch The Traveling Taylors YouTube videos. Stay immersed in the brand, the voice, and the content — it'll make every other task sharper.
  • ii.
    Research platforms. Look into how other travel creators are growing on YouTube, IG, Pinterest, TikTok. What hooks are landing? What formats are trending? Take notes.
  • iii.
    Come up with new strategies to test. Pitch new content formats, posting schedules, hashtag groups, hook templates, or platform features we haven't tried yet. Drop ideas in Blue or Slack so we can run experiments next month.
